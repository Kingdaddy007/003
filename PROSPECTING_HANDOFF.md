### Approved concept direction

**Working thesis:** The Architecture of Everyday Rituals.

Translate the studio's stated ideas - personal homes, natural materials, architectural detailing, and spaces that evolve with families - into a cinematic digital showroom. The experience should make visitors feel the renovation journey rather than simply browse a gallery.

Suggested sequence:

1. Quiet opening built around a lived-in ritual.
2. Material and joinery details as evidence of care.
3. Project reveal from architectural structure to human use.
4. Founder-led explanation of why the space feels personal.
5. Calm, qualified enquiry path for a considered residential renovation.
