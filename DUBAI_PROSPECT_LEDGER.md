## Screening vocabulary

- **Keep:** enough public evidence to inspect fully.
- **Hold:** plausible, but one important fact is missing or mixed.
- **Reject:** explicit ICP conflict, no real work, unreachable, or already too strong digitally.
- **Verify manually:** access or identity evidence is incomplete; this is not a claim that the studio is inactive or broke.
