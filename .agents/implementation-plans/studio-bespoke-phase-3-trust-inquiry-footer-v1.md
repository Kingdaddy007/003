# Studio Bespoke — Phase 3: Trust, Client Proof, Inquiry and Footer v1

**Status:** Design and content guide; begins only after Phase 2 approval.

**Scope:** Mira release → approach/trust → recognition/client proof → homepage inquiry → footer.

**Principle:** The lower homepage must decelerate. Mira is the last cinematic peak; trust and inquiry become quieter, clearer, and more useful.

## Structural recommendation

Do not delete trust entirely, but do not build a large standalone `Approach` spectacle. Merge Approach and Trust into one concise chapter, then follow it with a separate client/recognition chapter.

Final lower-page sequence:

1. `The work behind the feeling.` — concise method and responsibility.
2. `In other voices.` — Marie Claire Maison plus two client excerpts.
3. `Every home has a story.` — homepage inquiry.
4. Restrained footer.

This avoids repeating the Services section while still answering the high-value client's practical question: what will working together feel like?

## Chapter 6 — Approach / Trust

### Communication job

Move the visitor from visual admiration to confidence in the studio's judgment, care, and ability to carry a project through.

### Recommended concept — The Working Line

A warm-paper editorial field follows the final Mira room. One fine line travels vertically through three concise commitments. The line is not a decorative progress indicator; it represents continuity of responsibility.

Proposed commitments, subject to final copy approval:

1. `Listen closely.`

   The studio begins with how the client lives, not a preset look.
2. `Resolve completely.`

   Layout, detailing, materials and furnishings are developed as one whole.
3. `Carry it through.`

   Design coordination continues from concept through completion within the agreed scope.

These are editorial summaries of official About and Services language, not new guarantees.

### Composition

- Register: warm paper `#e8e0d4`, ink `#171a17`.
- One commanding heading at the upper left or centered-left; avoid another full-screen centered title card.
- Three commitments use an asymmetric editorial grid, not equal cards.
- A quiet evidence line may cite:
  - five-year accredited interior-design education;
  - experience in international and boutique design firms;
  - a trusted network of consultants, contractors and artisans.
- Credentials are studio-reported and should be attributed accordingly.

### Motion

- Ordinary document flow is preferred.
- Optional: the working line extends as each commitment enters; text itself remains stable.
- No pin, image sequence, horizontal transfer, or repeated bottom-to-top project reveal.
- Mobile: line becomes a left border with three naturally stacked commitments.
- Reduced motion: identical static composition.

## Chapter 7 — Recognition and client proof

### Communication job

Confirm authority already earned by the work. This section must not become a logo wall or testimonial carousel.

### Recommended concept — In Other Voices

Use one quiet editorial spread with two forms of proof:

- **Recognition:** Marie Claire Maison, linking to `https://maison.marieclairearabia.com/the-architecture-of-everyday-rituals/`.
- **Client experience:** two short exact excerpts from the official Studio Bespoke homepage.

### Recommended testimonial sources

Use client-facing testimony before former-colleague testimony:

- Julia Fetisova, Founder of New Earth Café and Al Barari homeowner.
- Jacqui Kee.

Leanne Rom is a valid reserve. Diane Thorsen's testimony is valuable professional endorsement but should not be presented as a client review.

Suggested excerpt strategy:

- Julia: retain the exact sentence about bringing ideas to life and attention to every detail.
- Jacqui: retain the exact sentence about direct guidance and seeing potential within an already planned space.
- Keep each excerpt short enough to read in one viewport.
- Preserve exact wording and attribution; do not paraphrase inside quotation marks.
- Add a source comment in the content data pointing to the official homepage.

### Composition and motion

- Recognition occupies a narrow side column or upper strip; it does not dominate the client voices.
- Two quotes form an editorial pair with generous measure and clear attribution.
- No star ratings, portrait avatars, autoplay, infinite ticker, or draggable carousel.
- A restrained text-mask transition is optional, but stillness is preferred after Mira.
- On mobile, present recognition followed by the two full excerpts in ordinary flow.

## Chapter 8 — Inquiry

### Communication job

Create a calm, selective threshold for a serious project conversation without sending the visitor to a second page.

### Copy direction

- Eyebrow: `Start a conversation`.
- Heading: `Every home has a story. Tell us where yours begins.`
- Support should preserve the official contact-page substance:
  - Studio Bespoke curates a limited number of projects each year.
  - thoughtful design takes time;
  - early inquiry is encouraged;
  - urgent one-to-two-month completion schedules may not be suitable depending on project size and scope.
- Primary action: `Share your project`.
- Avoid `Let's create something beautiful`, `Dream project`, or other generic luxury copy.

### Form design

Keep the complete inquiry on the homepage in a two-column field:

- left: invitation, availability posture, Dubai/contact summary;
- right: accessible form.

Recommended fields, pending confirmation against the production form contract:

- Name
- Email
- Phone / preferred contact
- Project location
- Project type
- Desired start date
- Approximate scope or budget band
- Project story / message
- Consent/privacy acknowledgement

Do not activate submission until the real destination, spam protection, privacy copy, validation contract, success state, and error state are confirmed.

### Contact-data gate

The public site currently contains inconsistent spellings:

- footer/homepage: `info@studiobespoke.design`;
- contact-page body: `info@studiobspoke.design`;
- social handle is also shown with the shorter spelling on the Contact page.

Treat `info@studiobespoke.design`, phone `+971 56 922 9240`, Instagram handle, and form destination as confirmation gates. Do not silently choose the typo or activate links before the owner confirms them.

### Interaction and accessibility

- Labels remain visible; placeholders do not replace labels.
- Focus states use ink/ivory contrast and are keyboard visible.
- Errors are field-specific and summarized at the top.
- Success replaces the form with a calm confirmation and expected response guidance.
- No cinematic motion, magnetic inputs, or cursor effects.

## Footer redesign

### Role

The footer closes the experience and provides orientation. It is not another advertising section.

### Composition

- Register: shared ink `#171a17` with ivory type.
- Oversized Studio Bespoke wordmark may anchor the base, but it remains static.
- Include only:
  - Work
  - Studio
  - Services
  - Inquiry
  - confirmed email/phone/social
  - Legal / Privacy
  - copyright
- Do not include a newsletter unless the studio confirms it is active and gives the real subscription destination.
- No kinetic wordmark, marquee, WebGL, giant hover reveal, or duplicated contact form.

## Whole-page color and register guide

Use one coherent palette instead of section-specific near-duplicates:

| Token | Value | Role |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Paper | `#e8e0d4` | Founder, chapter invitations, trust, inquiry |
| Ink | `#171a17` | Primary type and dark rooms |
| Ivory | `#f2eee5` | Type on dark rooms |
| Muted ink | `#6d655b` | Supporting copy on paper |
| Optional material accent | derive from approved project image | Rare rule or focus accent only |

Avoid the current mixture of `#11130f`, `#15130f`, `#181a16`, and unrelated beige surfaces. Project imagery may change visual temperature; the interface field should not.

## Acceptance criteria

- Approach adds trust without repeating the six service stages.
- Recognition is subordinate to the work and uses one real publication source.
- Testimonials use exact, attributed, official-site copy and no invented client claims.
- Inquiry exists on the homepage and communicates fit, timing, and next step clearly.
- No form or contact link is activated with unconfirmed data.
- Footer is complete, accessible, calm, and non-kinetic.
- The post-Mira page decelerates rather than attempting a fourth cinematic peak.
