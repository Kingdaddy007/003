# Brand Diagnostics: Studio Bespoke Design

**Last Updated:** 2026-07-14  
**Project target:** Studio Bespoke Design (Dubai, UAE)  
**Founder:** Brittany Guimaraes  

---

## 1. Brand Diagnosis

### Perception Gap
*The brand wants to be perceived as an elite interior architectural renovation studio that designs homes shaped by detail and craftsmanship, but the current footprint feels like a static, templated local contractor website because of broken functionality (counter widgets rendering as `0`, a dead Publications `#` navigation link) and flat, unpaced image grids that flatten the architectural storytelling of their high-end projects.*

### Taste World
- **Category:** Warm Residential Minimalism & Tactile Architecture.
- **Materials:** Raw limestone, micro-cement, oiled oak, linen fabrics, brass joinery detailing, textured plaster, and soft architectural concrete.
- **Light:** Dappled desert daylight, warm lamps casting structural shadows, soft wash light on raw textures.
- **Object/Furniture Language:** Sculptural organic chairs, custom built-in oak joinery, recessed shelving, and textured stone tables.
- **Photography Style:** High-end architectural photography, cropped tight on joints and material transitions, with soft human presence (a cup of tea on a table, sunlight shifting across a seat).
- **Typography Posture:** Restrained serif headlines combined with clean, wide-spaced sans-serif details.
- **UI Restraint:** Minimal borders, spacious margins, thin dividing lines, and hidden utility panels.
- **Motion Tolerance:** Slow, scroll-bound transitions mimicking a slow walkthrough, soft mask reveals, and smooth crossfades. No jittery bounces.
- **Inquiry Tone:** Understated, conversational concierge approach.

### The Enemy & Counter-Standard
- **The Enemy:** **Template Decor** (beige, standard serifs, stock luxury room images, and slide-up animations) and **Contractor Utility** (pure list of services, simple grids of projects, and no atmospheric storytelling).
- **Counter-Standard:** 
  *The old world says: "Luxury is a list of services and a collection of pretty pictures."*  
  *This studio believes: "A home is an organic sequence of daily rituals, and luxury is the precision of custom joinery and natural materials that frame those rituals."*  
  *That means the site must show: The design process, custom joinery details, and spatial flow through interactive scrolls and narrative case studies.*

### Premium Leaks (In Current Site)
- Counter widgets stuck at `0` (indicates neglect).
- Navigation items pointing to dead `#` paths (Publications).
- "Bespoke renovations" written in text without showing the tactile detailing of custom joinery.
- A standard grid of projects that looks like a stock portfolio rather than an authored journey.
- Email address mismatch on the contact page (`info@studiobspoke.design` vs domain spelling `studiobespoke.design`).

### Proof Architecture
- Curated case studies featuring Al Barari (six-bedroom villa), Desert Leaf, and Damac Hills.
- Detailed close-up imagery demonstrating custom cabinetry, material joints, and lighting integration.
- Process-driven text highlighting the "concept-to-completion" journey and family-centered evolution of spaces.

## 2. Redesign Brief

### Desired Perception
An elite, architectural interior design studio whose digital presence operates as a virtual gallery-showroom, commanding premium engagement rates and attracting clients who view home design as an investment in architectural craft.

### Visual Thesis Inputs
- **Sensation:** Stepping into a quiet, sunlit sanctuary away from the desert heat.
- **Signature Object/Detail:** The flush wooden joint or the raw limestone edge, showing where cabinetry meets stone.
- **Opening Event:** A slow horizontal split or screen wipe, simulating a large pocket door opening to reveal a sun-drenched corridor.
- **Inquiry Posture:** A calm, selective dialogue box that frames projects by scope and space type.

### Top Fixes
1. **Restore functional credibility:** Eliminate broken counters, correct the contact email spelling, and remove dead navigation hashes.
2. **Author the scroll:** Introduce structured narrative choreography that sequences the visitor through Atmosphere, Taste, Transformation, Proof, Method, and Inquiry.
3. **Elevate proof quality:** Move away from standard image lists to decision-based captions, showing the material selections and joinery details that make their Al Barari and Desert Leaf renovations unique.
