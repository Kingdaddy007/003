## 1. Studio Archetype & Stated Focus
- **Brand/Studio Type:** Boutique residential renovation and interior design studio.
- **Service Model:** Concept-to-completion turnkey delivery, custom joinery, and interior architecture.
- **Core Stated Philosophy:** Designing personal homes shaped by detail, natural materials, and craftsmanship—spaces that grow with families.

## 2. Target Audience & Desired Client
- **Primary Audience:** Affluent expatriates and local homeowners in premium Dubai communities (e.g., Al Barari, Damac Hills, Desert Leaf, Jumeirah).
- **Desired Client Persona:** Individuals or families undertaking full-scale residential renovations who value architectural detailing, custom joinery, and material integrity over quick, cheap cosmetic upgrades.
- **Client Motivations:** Desiring a home aligned with their daily rituals and spatial comfort; willing to pay for bespoke, long-term craftsmanship.

## 3. Price & Perception Goal
- **Perception Goal:** Position the studio as an elite, high-integrity spatial authority whose digital presence feels as premium and curated as a physical high-end residence.
- **Target Perception Price Signal:** Clear alignment with high-ticket ($50k–$200k+) residential interior architecture engagements.

## 4. Current Taste World
- **Taste World Characterization:** Warm, material-led luxury and structural restraint. It uses natural textures (plaster, stone, linen, wood) and focuses on the play of light.
- **Key Visual Signals:** Sculptural furniture, curated lighting, clean joinery lines, and open spatial sequences.

## 7. Wrong-Fit Clients
